Syrniki

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 1 people
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Steps:

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl

2. Heat a pan and pour the batter

3. Cook until golden brown on both sides

Kasha

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Grass 300 gr
Salt To taste
Butter 1 tbsp

Steps:

1. Bring milk (or water) to a boil.

2. Add the cereal and a pinch of salt.

3. Cook while stirring until soft.
